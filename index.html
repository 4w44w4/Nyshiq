Who Is NyshiQ

NyshiQ is a Las Vegas-born artist who makes music the way most people keep secrets — close, heavy, and unfiltered. Raised under the neon and the noise, his sound carries something the city doesn't advertise: the weight that lives beneath the lights.

His catalog reads like chapters in a story still being written. Nevada is the ground he came from. Mad is what it made him. Cold is what he became. Each project strips back another layer, trading spectacle for truth — bars built from actual experience, not borrowed pain.

His forthcoming EP, Letter to My Dead, marks a turning point. It's a direct conversation with loss — the people, the versions of himself, and the chapters that didn't survive. No detours. No performance. Just honesty given a beat.

Real pain in every bar. That's not a tagline. That's the whole thing.
